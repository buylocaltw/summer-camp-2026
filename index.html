關於本營隊About the Camp

不只是紙上談兵，
而是真正地「建造」

Not theory on paper —
actual construction.

「達文西橋」源自文藝復興時期的工程天才達文西，一座無需釘子、純靠結構力學咬合的木橋。

在這五天的營隊中，孩子將從零開始，理解橋梁的受力原理，親手丈量、切割、組裝，最終完成一座真實可站立的達文西橋，橫跨水面之上。

這不是模型，也不是展示品——而是孩子用自己的雙手完成的建築。

手作建築的過程涵蓋物理、數學與工程思維，但學習發生在行動之中，而非課本之上。完工的那一刻，是屬於他們自己的成就感——難以取代，也難以忘懷。

The Da Vinci Bridge originates from Renaissance genius Leonardo da Vinci — a wooden bridge held together purely by structural interlocking, without a single nail.

Over five days, campers start from scratch: grasping load-bearing principles, measuring, cutting, and assembling until a real, weight-bearing bridge spans the water.

This is not a model or a display piece — it is architecture completed by young hands.

The process weaves physics, mathematics, and engineering into action rather than textbooks. The moment of completion belongs entirely to them — irreplaceable, unforgettable.

Why choose

01
真實建造一座橋
Build a Real Bridge

從設計、備料、施工到完工，孩子全程親身參與，最終完成一座可承重、可渡河的達文西橋。不是工藝課，是真正的工程。

From design to materials to construction, campers are involved every step — completing a load-bearing Da Vinci Bridge. Not a craft class; real engineering.

02
了解科學原理
Learn the Science

橋梁為什麼不會垮？力是怎麼傳遞的？課程將物理、力學與幾何融入實作，讓孩子在動手的過程中自然理解原理。

Why doesn't a bridge collapse? How does force travel? Physics, mechanics, and geometry are woven into hands-on work so understanding emerges naturally.

03
高師生比，深度陪伴
Small Groups, Deep Mentorship

小組制、高師生比的設計，確保每位孩子都能獲得充分的關注與指導，遇到困難時有人一起思考、一起解決。

Small-group design with high instructor-to-student ratios ensures every child receives full attention. When problems arise, a mentor thinks and solves alongside them.

04
花蓮山水中的戶外學習
Outdoor Learning in Hualien

走出教室，在花蓮的自然環境中實作。戶外的學習場域讓孩子以全身感知尺度與空間，也在自然中培養專注與韌性。

Step out of the classroom and build within Hualien's natural landscape. The outdoor setting develops spatial awareness, focus, and resilience through the whole body.
